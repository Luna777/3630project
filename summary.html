Summary


Class is like a frame of a house. It is a basic construction of Java.

Main method is like a doorway of a house. From here, our program is starting everything.

Variables is like objects in a house. They can be used to do expression, calculation, etc.

Statements is like actions. They are used to create variables, assign values and make function/method calls.

Method is like a functional room in a house. It is holding some statements to do particular jobs, such as doing sum of two numbers, doing kitchen jobs of making food.

Grammar is the ordering, syntax, punctuations in Java. It is like cement of a house or the ordering of furniture. It puts words together and make them meaningful to computer.
